About



This is the "WARMEST" education conference for Physician Assistants in Utah!

Presented by the Utah Academy of Physician Assistants

Those who participate in this continuing medical education conference may be eligible to receive a maximum of 23 hours/credits for their attendance.

This events aims to offer informative, current, and relevant information for Physician Assistants devised to stimulate thinking through didactic lectures, lively debate, complex case studies and engaging workshops.

Invited faculty will present cutting-edge information on the diagnosis, management and treatment of common conditions, diseases, and problems.

Who should attend?



This education conference is primarily for Physician Assistants, though other medical professionals interested in an update on hot topics and information related to the daily needs of physician assistants, nurse practitioners, nurses, allied health clinicians, and students may find the information presented, relevant and useful to their current or future medical practice.

Accreditation Information



Continuing Medical Education Hours

PAs should only claim those hours actually spent participating in education sessions.

This program is not yet approved for CME credit. Conference organizers are in the process of requesting 23 hours of AAPA Category 1 CME from the Physician Assistant Review Panel. Total number of approved credits yet to be determined.

We expect to receive confirmation a couple weeks before the conference that the conference has been accredited.

Conference Cancellation Policy
Registration cancellation requests received by the 27th Annual UAPA John Allen Memorial Conference Registration office on or before Friday, March 11, 2016 will receive a refund, minus a $100 cancellation fee. Registration cancellation requests received after Friday, March 11, 2016 will not be refunded.
